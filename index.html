Why is it Important to learn another language in the U.S.?

English is the most spoken language in the United States and it is also true that the majority of citizens speak only english as their native language,however, sixty to seventy-five percent of people in the world speak more than one language. Although most of the world is either bilingual or trilingual and so on the United States citizens have a stigma that english should be the only language spoken. With this strong idea less natural born citizens know another language.As the United States becomes more and more diverse I believe there should be an app or website that help people learn another language in the comfort of their own home.
